RACING ROYALE
Designing and implementing the game
Stampede: Racing Royale – A thrilling kart racing and battle game with 60 participants, customization options, and endless events. Players can unlock new vehicles, customize their karts and characters, and participate in various challenges and events to win rewards.

With its classic circuit racing, battle arenas, and constant updates, Racing Royale promises endless excitement. Whether racing against 59 other opponents or joining a party with friends and family, there’s always something new to experience in this wild racing event by Tencent Games, one of the world’s largest video games businesses.
CLIENT

Tencent Games

ROLE

TOOLS & TECHNOLLOGIES

Challenge

Competing with the established Mario Kart series required adding more chaos and unique elements to stand out. Implementing 60-player races on varied circuits with potent power-ups demanded creativity and technical skill to create an engaging and competitive gaming experience. Balancing the simplicity of controls with the complexity of gameplay ensured it remained accessible yet challenging for players of all ages.

Solution

By introducing innovative gameplay elements and continuously updating content, Stampede: Racing Royale distinguishes itself from competitors. The balance of simple controls and complex gameplay ensures accessibility and enjoyment for players of all ages. Additionally, the cool user interface and engaging experience enhance the overall appeal, making it easy for players to immerse themselves in the game.

Design System

The design system for Stampede: Racing Royale focuses on a vibrant color scheme, simple controls, and engaging user interface elements to create an accessible and enjoyable gaming experience. Customization options and dynamic circuits enhance the game's appeal for players of all ages.

Desktop First Approach

In regard to the younger gamer target group, all UI designs are strongly made with a desktop-first approach, focusing on releases for Xbox and Steam. Mobile development is in progress to further extend the game's reach.

Prototype

This section showcases the prototypes used for the project, highlighting the design concepts and functionality. These prototypes are crafted for PC and HD, demonstrating our vision across high-definition platforms.

Result

The game turned out to be a success on Steam after its launch on both Xbox and Steam, achieving a Very Positive rating of 85%. This impressive reception highlights the game's widespread appeal and strong player satisfaction.To further enhance these results, work is in progress on the mobile version to reach a broader audience and achieve even better reception.
