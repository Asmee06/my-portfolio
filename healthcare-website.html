HEALTHCARE WEBSITE
Responsive healthcare website user journey
Kaiser Permanente is dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable, and accessible health care services, focusing on improving the health of its members and the populations it serves.

The organization emphasizes preventative care, personalized treatments, mental health support, community engagement, and online resources to ensure holistic care for all patients, enhancing their overall well-being. Kaiser Permanente continually strives to innovate and advance healthcare to meet the evolving needs of its members.
CLIENT

Kaiser Permanente

ROLE

Senior UI Designer-Developer

LOCATION

Bangalore, India

TOOLS & TECHNOLLOGIES

Figma, AEM, Angular 9, Ajax, HTML5, Bootstrap, CSS3,JSON, GIT

Challenge

Designing a healthcare website requires intuitive navigation, accessibility features, and a balance between aesthetics and functionality while ensuring integration with other systems and maintaining fast loading speeds for mobile users.The design must accommodate a diverse audience, including patients and healthcare providers, with clear layouts, readable fonts, and seamless integration with electronic health records and telemedicine platforms.

Solution

By introducing innovative gameplay elements and continuously updating content, Stampede: Racing Royale distinguishes itself from competitors. The balance of simple controls and complex gameplay ensures accessibility and enjoyment for players of all ages. Additionally, the cool user interface and engaging experience enhance the overall appeal, making it easy for players to immerse themselves in the game.

Design System

The design system for Stampede: Racing Royale focuses on a vibrant color scheme, simple controls, and engaging user interface elements to create an accessible and enjoyable gaming experience. Customization options and dynamic circuits enhance the game's appeal for players of all ages.

Desktop First Approach

In regard to the younger gamer target group, all UI designs are strongly made with a desktop-first approach, focusing on releases for Xbox and Steam. Mobile development is in progress to further extend the game's reach.

Prototype

Elaborate a complete analysis of the current platform and Ganbatte processes and bring a solution aligning business with the market and users needs.

Result

The game turned out to be a success on Steam after its launch on both Xbox and Steam, achieving a Very Positive rating of 85%. This impressive reception highlights the game's widespread appeal and strong player satisfaction.To further enhance these results, work is in progress on the mobile version to reach a broader audience and achieve even better reception.
